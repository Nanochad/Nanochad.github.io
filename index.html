About

I am in the final year of my MSc in Computer Science at NOVA School of Science & Technology in Lisbon, specialising in distributed systems and cybersecurity, after a completed BSc in Informatics Engineering.

What I enjoy most is the part of computing that is genuinely hard: getting machines to agree (consensus), keeping data correct and available when things fail, and computing on data without giving up its confidentiality.
